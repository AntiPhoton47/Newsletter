# Story Scorecard

Use this template to evaluate candidate stories before deciding whether they belong in the newsletter and where they belong.

## How To Use

1. Create one copy of the template for each candidate story.
2. Score each category from `1` to `5`.
3. Add short notes rather than long prose.
4. Use the total score as a guide, not as an automatic decision rule.
5. Apply the tailoring rules from [selection_criteria.md](/Users/munga/PycharmProjects/Newsletter/selection_criteria.md) when scores are close.

## Suggested Thresholds

- `34-40`: strong candidate for main entry
- `27-33`: likely short take or backup main entry
- `20-26`: include only if needed for section balance
- `<20`: usually exclude

## Fast Heuristics

### Main Entry

Use as a main entry if most of the following are true:

- high relevance
- strong conceptual payoff
- durable beyond the current news cycle
- supported by a strong source
- materially improves the issue

### Short Take

Use as a short take if most of the following are true:

- useful but not central
- time-sensitive or incremental
- complements a section without dominating it
- can be explained in one or two lines

### Exclude

Usually exclude if most of the following are true:

- low novelty
- weak sourcing
- mostly hype or repetition
- minimal conceptual or practical payoff
- redundant with a stronger story already selected
